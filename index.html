Why do we want to look in our Belly Buttons?

1. You never know what you can find in there! Maybe your belly button grows little animals that are worth money somewhere...or can produce meet?! This project is all about a study using volunteers to see if their belly buttons can produce little microorganisms that could be sold to make so-called meatless proteins

2. For volunteers who are interested in selling their bacteria to Improbable Beef, please contact the study...but I am not going to tell you here how to do that! several ways to visualize the types of bacteria that colonize our volunteer bellies. Some of them will be suitable for synthetic beef production, while others will not.

3. Who knows what the next consumer fashion will be! Just in time for April Fools Day: a new holiday is defined:Belly Button Biome Day. I can only imagine. Just think, we can make belly button plushies containing plush little microorganisms for young scientists.
